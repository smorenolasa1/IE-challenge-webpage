Morning Routine

Brushing Teeth

Start your day by brushing your teeth. Use a soft toothbrush and toothpaste with fluoride. Brush for at least two minutes, making sure to clean all sides of your teeth and your tongue.

Eating Breakfast

Having a nutritious breakfast is important. Eat a balanced meal that includes foods like whole grains, fruits, and dairy. This will give you the energy you need for the day ahead.
