Evening Routine

Going to School

As part of your evening routine, make sure to prepare for the next day of school. Pack your backpack with all the necessary supplies, complete your homework, and choose your outfit for the next day.

Family Dinner

Enjoy a nutritious family dinner together. Discuss your day, share stories, and spend quality time with your loved ones during the evening meal.

Reading Time

Allocate some time in your evening routine for reading. Choose a book that interests you, whether it's fiction, non-fiction, or educational. Reading before bedtime can be a relaxing and enjoyable activity.

Screen Time Limits

Be mindful of your screen time in the evening. Set limits on electronic devices and avoid using screens at least an hour before bedtime to ensure better sleep quality.
